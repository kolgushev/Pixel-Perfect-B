    REQUIRES normal.rgb
    REQUIRES lightmap.rgb
    REQUIRES AO data (lightmap.b)
    [if pixelated shadows] REQUIRES normal

defer1: combine passes and fog, manually clear albedo.rgba
    REQUIRES sky.rgb
    REQUIRES albedo.rgb
    REQUIRES sky mask (albedo.a)
